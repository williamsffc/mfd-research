Who We Are

Strategic Partners in Clinical Excellence

MFD Research is a specialized consulting firm built on over 18 years of hands-on clinical research leadership. We partner with pharmaceutical companies, biotech firms, and research sponsors to design, build, and optimize high-performing research operations.

Our founder brings direct site-level experience across every stage of a trial's lifecycle — from regulatory setup and staff training to quality management and sponsor relations. We understand what it takes to run a site that sponsors trust and regulators approve.

Whether you're launching a first-in-human study, expanding your therapeutic portfolio, or remediating a struggling site, we bring the operational expertise to move fast and execute with precision.
